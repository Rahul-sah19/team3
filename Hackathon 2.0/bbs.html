What is BBS?

BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) is an undergraduate program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of business concepts and practices. The course covers various subjects such as management, accounting, economics, marketing, finance, business law, and organizational behavior. BBS programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for managing and leading businesses in a dynamic and competitive global environment. The curriculum typically includes both theoretical learning and practical experiences, such as internships, case studies, and projects. Graduates of the BBS program are well-prepared for careers in business management, entrepreneurship, marketing, finance, and human resource management, among other fields. It also provides a solid foundation for further studies in business administration, such as pursuing an MBA.
